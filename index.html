About us.

At piKopi, we're not just about coffee; we're about creating memorable moments, fostering connections, and indulging in the simple pleasures of life. Our passion for the perfect cup of coffee drives us to source the finest beans, carefully craft each brew, and provide a warm and inviting space for you to savor it.

Our story.

Founded in 2023, piKopi was born out of a deep love for coffee and a desire to share that love with our community. We've always believed that great coffee should be accessible to everyone, and that's why we've dedicated ourselves to serving exceptional coffee at affordable prices.
